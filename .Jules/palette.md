## 2026-04-15 - SVG Tooltip Accessibility\n**Learning:** Custom CSS-based tooltips in SVG paths often lack accessibility contexts like focusability, visual focus styles, and screen reader announcements. Adding `tabindex="0"` and `aria-label` to target paths, alongside `:focus` and `:focus-visible` states to the tooltip elements, allows screen readers and keyboard users to successfully interact with visually hidden tooltips in data maps.\n**Action:** Always ensure that dynamically injected visual tooltips come coupled with semantic focusability, accessible descriptions (`aria-label` or `aria-describedby`), and explicitly declared focus styles.
